Site visit: Inside Infinitev (Auckland, NZ) - The Best EV Battery Refurbishment Process

Visiting Infinitev, one of NZ and Australasia’s leading hybrid and EV battery specialists, was an eye-opening experience.
Based in Auckland, Infinitev has been repairing and remanufacturing hybrid and electric vehicle batteries since 2015, helping extend battery lifespans, reduce e-waste, and promote sustainable transport solutions.
This visit offered a rare behind-the-scenes look into how used and degraded EV batteries are refurbished, repurposed, and recycled.
Transforming what many consider “waste” into valuable, functional components once again.

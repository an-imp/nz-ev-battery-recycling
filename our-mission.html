Our mission & Goal

Our mission is to build a sustainable, circular framework for electric vehicle (EV) battery recycling in New Zealand â€” one that protects the environment, strengthens the economy, and supports the country's transition to low-carbon transport.

We aim to provide a structured and practical model that integrates government policy, industry collaboration, and technological innovation to manage end-of-life (EoL) EV batteries safely and efficiently.

Our Goals

  • 1. Promote Environmental Responsibility
  • 2. Encourage Innovation and Technology Development
  • 3. Strengthen Economic Opportunities
  • 4. Shape Effective Policy and Collaboration

Our Vision

Aotearoa New Zealand will become a regional leader in sustainable EV battery management, closing the loop between use, reuse, and recycling. Through this framework, we envision a future where every battery contributes to cleaner transport, renewable energy growth, and a thriving circular economy.

01
A Circular Battery Economy for Aotearoa

Establish a closed-loop system where every EV battery is reused, repurposed, or recycled, minimising waste and protecting natural resources.

02
A Green and Resilient Future

Strengthen New Zealand's energy resilience through second-life battery use, supporting renewable energy integration and reducing carbon emissions.

03
Global Leadership in Sustainable Innovation

Position New Zealand as a regional leader in clean-tech innovation and responsible EV battery management through collaboration, research, and policy excellence.
